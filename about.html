About ResumeGen

Helping students and professionals build clean, ATS-friendly resumes with ease.

Who We Are

ResumeGen is a simple and powerful resume builder designed to help job seekers create professional resumes quickly. We focus on clean design, clarity, and industry standards so your resume stands out to recruiters and Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Whether you are a fresher, a student, or an experienced professional, our single professional resume template is crafted to work across industries and job roles.

Our Story

Founded by a small team of recruiters and designers, ResumeGen was created to remove the guesswork from resume writing. We combine best-practice formatting with a clean visual style so you can focus on your experience and achievements.

What We Offer

  • ATS-friendly layout and clear sectioning
  • Quick, guided inputs to highlight your strengths
  • Downloadable PDF ready for job applications
